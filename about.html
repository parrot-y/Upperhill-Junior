Our Academic Philosophy

At Upperhill Junior Academy, we move beyond rote memorization. Our approach is rooted in the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), focusing on what the learner can actually do rather than just what they know.

Learner-Centered Approach

We believe every child is unique. Our small class sizes allow teachers to provide personalized attention, ensuring no student is left behind.

Holistic Development

Education at Upperhill extends beyond the four walls of a classroom. From the swimming pool to the music room, we provide diverse platforms for talent discovery.

Technology in Education
